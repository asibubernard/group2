    Welcome To Big7 Informatics - Group-2

    Big7 Informatics is a revolutionary health information management firm that was established and is run by a vibrant group of students who are passionate about transforming the healthcare industry with cutting-edge solutions. We are a group of enthusiastic people dedicated to improving the management, examination, and defence of crucial health information, making sure it is accurate, secure, and available when and where it is required.

    Services

    Health Data Management:

    Data Collection: Gathering health data from various sources. Data Processing: Organizing and managing health data. Data Analysis: Analyzing health data to derive meaningful insights.

    Health Informatics

    Developing Health Information Systems: Creating systems to manage patient data, hospital management, etc. EHR (Electronic Health Record) Management: Managing and optimizing the use of EHR systems. Telehealth Services: Developing and managing platforms for remote healthcare services.

    Health Data Analytics

    Predictive Analytics: Using health data to predict trends and outcomes. Population Health Management: Analyzing data to improve health outcomes at the population level. Clinical Data Analysis: Analyzing clinical data to support healthcare providers and research.
